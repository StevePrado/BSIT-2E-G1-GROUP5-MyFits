The Problem

“I have nothing to wear.”

We’ve all had that moment where we stare at a full closet and can't decide what to wear. The problem isn't a lack of clothes; it's a lack of organization. We often forget what we own because it’s buried in drawers, making it hard to mix and match outfits efficiently.

The Solution

Your "Digital Twin"

My Fits is a web-based application that acts as a "Digital Twin" of your closet. It helps users organize their wardrobe, plan outfits, and save time getting ready in the morning—all from their browser.
